Mac and Cheese for Odin for Odin

Delicious cheesy mac and cheese

Heartwarming cheesy mac and cheese made from the milk of unicorns, perfect for thanksgiving and christmas

    Macaroni Unicorn Meat Dwarf Cheese Water Unicorn Milk
  1. Put Macaroni in boiling unicorn tears
  2. Mix dwarf cheese and unicorn milk in pot
  3. Let cheese and milk mixture heat up and mix into eachother
  4. Take cooked macaroni and cheese mixture and combine
  5. Put on ceramic plate and serve to guest
