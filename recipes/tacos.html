Tacos for Odin Himself

Delicious exotic type tacos made from the penguins of happy feet

  • Tortillas
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Penguin Meat
  • lettuce
  1. Brown ground penuin meat in a pan
  2. Add taco seasoning to penguin meat
  3. Deep fry tortillas in bear fat
  4. Combine all ingredients together until it looks like a taco
  5. Enjoy!
